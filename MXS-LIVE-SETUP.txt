MOTOXSYNDICATE MXS LIVE - GOOGLE SHEET CONTROL PANEL
=====================================================

Your live page is controlled from the published Google Sheet.
After this website version is deployed, normal broadcast updates do NOT
require editing code, using VS Code, pushing GitHub, or redeploying Cloudflare.
The page checks the sheet about every 10 seconds.

SHEET LAYOUT
------------
Keep labels in column A and values in column B. Use these rows:

A1  Event Name            B1  TEST Event
A2  Race Date             B2  July 19, 2026
A3  Race Time             B3  7:00 PM

IMPORTANT
---------
1. Keep the sheet published to the web as CSV.
2. Do not rename the labels in column A unless they match the names above.
3. Press Enter after editing a value.
4. Changes normally appear within about 10 seconds.
5. Class, Format, and Server are controlled by rows 14, 15, and 16.
